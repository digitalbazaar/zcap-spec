Linked Data Capabilities (ld-ocap for short) provide a secure way for linked data systems to grant and express authority utilizing the object capability model. Capabilities are expressed through "proclamation chains" which are themselves linked data objects signed through Linked Data Signatures. Linked Data Capabilities support delegating authority to other entities on the network. "Caveats" may be attached to proclamations which may be used to restrict the scope of their use, for example to restrict the methods which may be used or providing a mechanism by which the capability may be later revoked.

This specification is currently being drafted to be considered as a work item for the Community Credentials Group.
